Security Measures

At Agile Bank, we prioritize the security of our customers' accounts and transactions. Here are some important security measures and tips:

  • Customer Complaints Lodgement: If you encounter any issues or unauthorized transactions, you can lodge a complaint through our Customer Grievance Redressal System (CGRS). You can also contact the National Helpline at 180042500000.
  • Cyber Security Awareness: Stay informed about cyber threats and how to protect yourself online. Regularly update your antivirus software and avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading unknown attachments.
  • Online Complaints: You can register complaints online through our CGRS portal. We take all complaints seriously and aim to resolve them promptly.
  • Block Digital Transactions: In case of fraudulent digital transactions, you can block all digital channels by sending an SMS with the keyword "BLOCK" to 092310 00001.
  • Reporting Of Cyber Crime Incidents: If you become a victim of cybercrime or unauthorized transactions, report the incident to your home branch and the cyber police. You can also register online complaints with the cyber police at https://www.cybercrime.gov.in/.
